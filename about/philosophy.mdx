## Nodes, Not Layers

Traditional tools organize work in **layers** — linear, hierarchical. Canopy AI organizes work in **nodes** — a graph model that lets you think in any direction.

## Fluidity of Connection

Connections between nodes — **noodles** — can be arranged and rearranged freely:
- A text concept becomes an image
- An image becomes a video
- A video gets scored with generated audio
- The whole pipeline feeds back for iteration

Nothing is locked. Everything flows.
