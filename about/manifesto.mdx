## Creation Should Feel Natural

The creative process and nature are both inherently **non-linear and organic**. We built Canopy AI to combine **creative ease and creative control**.

Current AI creative tools are made by non-creatives for other non-creatives to feel creative. Professional creatives need something different.

Canopy AI emerged from **NYU ITP** — an art and technology graduate program — as an applied human-computer interaction company.

By treating each modality as an independent node capable of non-linear networking, we liberate the creative process from hierarchical constraints. This enables divergent thinking for exploration and convergent organization for production.

> **Plant your ideas. Watch them grow.**
