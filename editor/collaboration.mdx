FLORA is built for teams. Multiple people can edit the same canvas simultaneously, and every change appears in real time for everyone in the project — no version conflicts, no manual syncing. Whether you're reviewing a campaign with a client or iterating on a workflow with a colleague, everyone always sees the most current version of your work.

## Real-Time Canvas Editing

Any number of collaborators can work on the same project at the same time. Changes appear live for all participants as nodes are added, moved, connected, and generated.

## Annotation Tool

Leave text comment bubbles anywhere on the canvas to give feedback, flag ideas, or ask questions in context — right alongside the nodes they relate to.

To place a comment:
1. Click the **Comment** button in the toolbar to activate the comment cursor.
2. Click anywhere on the canvas to drop a text bubble.
3. Type your comment and submit.

Teammates see your comment in real time and can reply or resolve it directly on the canvas.

## Sharing a Project

### Copy Link

To share a project with someone, navigate to the **parameter bar** in the top-right corner of the canvas and click **Copy Link**. Send that link to anyone you want to invite.

### Workspace Roles

Access is governed by workspace roles:

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage members, settings, billing, and all content |
| **Editor** (Can Edit) | Create, edit, and run projects; cannot manage workspace settings |
| **Guest** (Can View) | Read-only access to shared projects; cannot edit or run nodes |

### Publish to Community

Publishing a project makes it visible to all FLORA users on the **Community tab** at [app.flora.ai/community](https://app.flora.ai/community). Community projects can inspire other creators and showcase your work publicly.

To publish:
1. Navigate to the **parameter bar** in the top-right corner of the canvas.
2. Click **Publish to Community**.
