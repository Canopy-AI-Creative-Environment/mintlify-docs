## What Are Elements?

Elements are reusable visual identity references that you attach to generation nodes. Upload a reference image of a character, product, or style, and Canopy AI will maintain consistency every time that Element is used -- across nodes, projects, and team members.

<Tip>Elements work best with 3-5 reference images showing different angles or lighting conditions.</Tip>

## Creating and Using Elements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Element">
    Open the Elements panel from the sidebar, click **+ New Element**, upload reference images, and give it a descriptive name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach to a Node">
    Drag an Element onto any Image or Video node, or select it from the Element dropdown in the node's settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate with Consistency">
    Run your workflow as usual -- the model will reference your Element to maintain visual identity across outputs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Element Types

| Type | Use Case |
|---|---|
| **Character** | Maintain a consistent face and body across scenes |
| **Product** | Keep branding, shape, and color accurate |
| **Style** | Apply a consistent artistic treatment to any output |
