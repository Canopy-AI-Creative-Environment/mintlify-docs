## Overview

The Timeline Editor brings multi-track video editing directly into your Canopy AI canvas. Arrange video clips, audio tracks, and transitions on a familiar horizontal timeline without leaving the app. Open it by selecting a Video Node output and clicking **Open in Timeline**.

<Note>The Timeline Editor works with any media generated on the canvas -- no need to export and re-import.</Note>

## Working with Tracks

Add multiple video and audio tracks by clicking the **+** button on the track header. Drag clips to reorder them, trim edges by dragging clip handles, and split clips with `S`. Each track supports independent volume and opacity controls.

## Exporting from the Timeline

When your edit is ready, click **Export** to render the final composition. Choose resolution, frame rate, and format, then route the output to an Export Node or back into the canvas for further AI processing.

<Tip>You can feed a timeline export back into a Video Node for AI-powered style transfer on your edited sequence.</Tip>
