Full price breakdown for every Image and Video model whose cost changes with resolution, duration, or other settings. For the at-a-glance list of every model's default-settings price, see [Model Pricing](https://docs.flora.ai/plans-and-billing/model-pricing).

**Pricing is dynamic — your cost changes with the parameters you choose.** Each table below shows the price for every combination of cost-affecting settings, computed from the model's base rate and its published per-parameter modifiers. The exact cost for your specific parameters — including batch count — is always shown in the app before you generate. Models not listed here generate at a single flat rate (see [Model Pricing](https://docs.flora.ai/plans-and-billing/model-pricing)).
