### 

**Overview**

Google's most powerful image model with premium 4K generation and multi-image composition capabilities.

---

### 

**Quick facts**

- **Modes:** Text → Image · Image → Image (editing) · Images → Image (multi-image blend)
 
- **Default output / size:** 1024×1024 (1:1)
 
- **Aspect ratios:** 21:9, 16:9, 5:4, 4:3, 3:2, 1:1, 2:3, 3:4, 4:5, 9:16
 
- **Resolutions:** 1K, 2K, 4K
 

---

### 

**What it's great for**

- Premium quality image generation at up to 4K resolution.
 
- Professional multi-image composition (up to 14 input images).
 
- Highest fidelity outputs for commercial and professional use.
 
- Complex editing and inpainting tasks.
