## What Are Techniques?

Techniques are pre-built multi-step AI workflows in Canopy AI. Each Technique is a complete node graph that performs a specific creative task -- from generating product photography to creating social media video series. Browse the library, run a Technique with one click, or fork it to customize.

<Tip>Open the Techniques panel from the sidebar or visit [app.trycanopy.space](https://app.trycanopy.space) to browse the full library.</Tip>

## How Techniques Work

A Technique is a saved canvas configuration: nodes, connections, prompts, and settings packaged as a reusable template. When you run a Technique, Canopy AI recreates the workflow on your canvas and executes it with your inputs.

## Categories

| Category | Examples |
