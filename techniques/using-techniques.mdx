## Finding Techniques

Browse Techniques from the sidebar panel or the Canopy AI homepage at [app.trycanopy.space](https://app.trycanopy.space). Filter by category, sort by popularity or recency, and preview the node graph before running.

## Running a Technique

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select">Click on a Technique to see its description, required inputs, and expected outputs.</Step>
  <Step title="Configure Inputs">Fill in the required fields -- text prompts, upload images, or select Elements.</Step>
  <Step title="Run">Click **Run Technique**. The workflow executes and outputs appear on your canvas.</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>After running a Technique, all its nodes remain on your canvas. You can tweak prompts and re-run individual nodes without restarting the entire workflow.</Tip>

## Customizing Techniques

Every Technique is fully editable once it lands on your canvas. Add nodes, change models, adjust prompts, swap Elements, or rewire connections. Save your modified version as a new Technique to reuse later.

<Note>Customizing a community Technique creates a private copy in your workspace -- your changes do not affect the original.</Note>
