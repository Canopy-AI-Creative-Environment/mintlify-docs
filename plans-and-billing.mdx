## Teams

Each seat adds cost and budget. Self-serve plans support up to **8 seats**. Admins can pool or split budgets and cap individual members.

## On-Demand Spending

When budget runs out, enable on-demand spending — same rates, no markup, with a cap you set.

## Managing Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open workspace settings">Click the gear icon in the workspace header.</Step>
  <Step title="Go to People & Seats">Select People & Seats in the sidebar.</Step>
  <Step title="Manage & Invoices">Opens the Stripe billing portal.</Step>
</Steps>

- **Upgrades** — immediate, prorated
- **Downgrades** — apply at end of cycle
