## What Is an Organization?

An Organization groups multiple Canopy AI workspaces under a single admin and billing structure. Ideal for agencies, studios, and companies managing separate client or team workspaces.

<Tip>Organizations are available on the Team plan and above. Contact prateek@justvouch.club to get started.</Tip>

## Organization Features

- **Unified billing** -- one invoice for all workspaces
- **Central admin** -- manage members across workspaces from a single dashboard
- **SSO support** -- enable single sign-on for your organization (Enterprise plan)
- **Usage reporting** -- aggregate credit usage across all workspaces

## Setting Up an Organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create">Go to **Account Settings > Organizations** and click **Create Organization**.</Step>
  <Step title="Add Workspaces">Create new workspaces or move existing ones into the organization.</Step>
  <Step title="Assign Admins">Designate organization-level admins who can manage all workspaces.</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Workspace-level settings (members, projects, Elements) remain independent within the organization.</Note>
