## IP Ownership

You own everything you create with Canopy AI. All generated images, videos, audio, and text are yours -- Canopy AI claims no ownership or license over your outputs. Use them commercially, publish them, or resell them without restriction.

<Note>This applies to all plans, including the free tier. Your outputs are yours.</Note>

## Data Security

Canopy AI employs industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

- **Encryption in transit** -- all data transmitted over TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at rest** -- stored files and database records encrypted with AES-256
- **Access controls** -- role-based permissions at workspace and organization levels
- **Infrastructure** -- hosted on SOC 2 compliant cloud providers

## Data Handling

| Data Type | Retention | Used for Training? |
|---|---|---|
| **Uploaded files** | Retained while your account is active | No |
| **Generated outputs** | Retained while your account is active | No |
| **Prompts** | Retained for workflow re-runs | No |
| **Account data** | Retained per our privacy policy | No |

<Tip>Canopy AI does not use your data to train models. Your prompts, uploads, and outputs are never shared with model providers for training purposes.</Tip>
