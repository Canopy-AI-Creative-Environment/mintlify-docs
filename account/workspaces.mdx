## What Is a Workspace?

A workspace is your home in Canopy AI. It holds your projects, Elements, Techniques, API keys, and billing settings. Every Canopy AI account starts with one personal workspace.

<Tip>Use separate workspaces to keep client projects isolated -- each workspace has its own billing, members, and data.</Tip>

## Workspace Settings

Access workspace settings from the gear icon in the sidebar. Here you can:

- **Rename** your workspace
- **Manage members** -- invite, remove, and change roles
- **View usage** -- track generation credits and storage
- **Billing** -- update your plan, payment method, and view invoices

## Creating Additional Workspaces

Upgrade to a Starter plan or above to create multiple workspaces. Each workspace operates independently with its own member list, projects, and credit balance.

<Note>Need to manage multiple workspaces under one billing umbrella? See [Organizations](/account/organizations).</Note>
