FLORA workspaces support multiple users so your whole creative team can collaborate on projects with shared or separate usage budgets. You can have up to **8 seats** on a self-serve plan, with each new seat adding both cost to your subscription and budget to your workspace's usage pool. For teams larger than 8, or for teams that need SSO and invoice billing, see [Organizations](/account/organizations).

## Adding Seats

To add team members, go to **Settings → People & Seats** and click **Invite**. You can invite by email address or by copying a shareable invite link. Each person you add occupies one seat on your plan.

<Note>
  Self-serve workspaces support up to **8 seats**. If you need more, contact [support@florafauna.ai](mailto:support@florafauna.ai) to explore Enterprise options.
</Note>

## Budget Modes

By default, all team members draw from a single shared pool. Admins can change this at any time from **Settings → People & Seats → Plan & Usage**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pooled (Default)">
    All team members share one usage budget. The first person to generate uses from the pool, and everyone else draws from the same balance. This is the simplest setup and works well for small, collaborative teams.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Per-User">
    Each member gets their own individual usage budget for the billing period. Use this mode when teammates have separate clients or cost centers and you want clear per-person accountability.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Per-Member Spending Caps

Regardless of which budget mode you choose, you can set a dollar cap on any individual team member. Once a member reaches their cap, their generations pause until the budget resets or you raise the limit.

<Tip>
  Use per-member caps to prevent a single teammate's heavy usage from draining the shared pool or accumulating unexpected overage charges.
</Tip>

To set a cap, go to **Settings → People & Seats**, find the member's row, and enter a cap amount.

## On-Demand Spending (Overage)

When your workspace's monthly usage budget runs out, generations stop — unless you've opted in to on-demand (overage) spending. Overage lets your team keep creating and charges the additional usage at the same published rates with no markup.

<Accordion title="How on-demand billing works">
  On-demand spending is **opt-in per workspace**. To enable it, go to **Settings → People & Seats → Plan & Usage** and turn on **On-Demand Spending**. You can also set a **maximum overage cap** for the billing period to keep costs predictable.

  Once enabled, your workspace is charged when any of the following thresholds is reached — not only at the end of the billing cycle:

  - Your max overage cap is reached
  - Unpaid overage reaches **$200**
  - Unpaid overage reaches your workspace's monthly subscription total
  - A daily check finds more than **$5** in unpaid overage

  Charges use the same published per-model rates — there is no markup for on-demand usage.
</Accordion>

All team management lives in **Settings → People & Seats**. From there you can:

## Upgrading to Enterprise

Self-serve teams max out at 8 seats and use Stripe card billing. If you need more, FLORA's Enterprise tier adds:

- **Custom seat count** — no upper limit on team size
- **SSO** — connect your identity provider for centralized login management
- **Audit logs** — full activity history for compliance and security review
- **Invoice billing** — net-60 invoicing instead of per-cycle card charges
- **Multiple workspaces** — manage all workspaces under one bill via Organizations

<Card title="Learn About Organizations" icon="building" href="/account/organizations">
  Enterprise customers can group multiple workspaces under one account with shared seats, a shared usage pool, and a single bill.
</Card>
