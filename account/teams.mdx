## Inviting Team Members

Open **Workspace Settings > Members** and click **Invite**. Enter email addresses and select a role. Invitees receive an email with a link to join your workspace at [app.trycanopy.space](https://app.trycanopy.space).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Members">Go to Workspace Settings and click the Members tab.</Step>
  <Step title="Invite">Click **Invite Member**, enter the email, and choose a role.</Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">The invitee accepts via email and gains access to the workspace.</Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Viewer** | View projects and outputs |
| **Editor** | Create and edit projects, run workflows |
| **Admin** | All Editor permissions plus member management and billing |
| **Owner** | Full control including workspace deletion |

## Managing Seats

Your plan determines the number of included seats. Additional seats can be purchased from **Workspace Settings > Billing**. Remove a member at any time to free up a seat.

<Note>Removing a member does not delete their contributions -- all projects and outputs remain in the workspace.</Note>
