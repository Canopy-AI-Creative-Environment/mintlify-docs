Canopy AI is the first AI-powered creative workflow system built for professionals who think in systems, not just outputs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for Canopy AI">
    Go to [app.trycanopy.space](https://app.trycanopy.space) and create your account with **Google** or **email**. Your workspace is set up automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the canvas">
    Click **New Project**. You land on the **infinite canvas** — a blank, zoomable workspace.

    | Action | Shortcut |
    |--------|----------|
    | Pan | Space + drag |
    | Zoom | Scroll wheel / trackpad |
    | Fit to view | F |
    | Command palette | Cmd K / Ctrl K |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first node">
    **Double-click** anywhere on the canvas to open the node picker. Select a model — try an **Image** node like **Flux 2** — and type a prompt.

    <Note>
      On the **Free plan**, you have access to text and image models. Video and audio require Starter or above.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect nodes with noodles">
    Hover over a node to see the **+ handle** on the right edge. Drag from that handle to another node's input. Data flows automatically when you run.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first generation">
    Press **Enter** on the node or click **Run**. Canopy AI streams the result back in real time.

    <Tip>
      The **flower meter** next to Run shows approximate cost. Switch to exact dollars in **Preferences → Show generation costs in dollars**.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
