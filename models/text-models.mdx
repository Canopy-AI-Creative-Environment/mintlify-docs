FLORA's Text Node connects your workflows to the most capable language models from Anthropic, Google, and OpenAI. Whether you feed it a plain text prompt, an image, a video clip, or an audio file, the Text Node produces polished written output — making it your go-to block for content generation, analysis, summarization, transcription, and reasoning tasks.

## Choosing the Right Model

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Speed Priority">
    Reach for **GPT 4o Mini** (20s) or **Claude Sonnet 4.5** (25s) when you need fast turnarounds — ideal for iterative drafting, chatbot prototypes, and real-time feedback loops inside larger workflows.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Quality Priority">
    **Gemini 3.1 Pro** and **GPT-5.2** are best when accuracy and output fidelity matter most. Expect slightly longer generation times in exchange for significantly richer results.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Multimodal Input">
    **Gemini 3 Flash** and **Gemini 2.5 Pro** excel when your Text Node receives image, video, or audio inputs from upstream nodes. Both handle cross-modal reasoning out of the box.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Research & Analysis">
    Use **o3 Deep Research** for tasks that demand thorough investigation across multiple sources — detailed competitive analysis, legal reviews, or academic summaries.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

</Tip>
