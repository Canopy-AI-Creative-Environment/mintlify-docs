FLORA's Audio Node brings voice, music, and sound effects into your creative workflows. Connect it to text nodes to produce voiceovers, feed it audio files for transcription, or use it as a terminal node to generate background music and sound effects. Audio outputs are delivered as MP3 files and can be routed into any downstream node — including video models that accept audio input for lipsync or audio-driven generation.

<Note>
  Audio models are available on **all plans, including Free**. You don't need a paid subscription to start generating audio in FLORA.
</Note>

## Model Details

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Text-to-Speech">
    FLORA offers two TTS models with complementary strengths:

    **ElevenLabs Multilingual v2** is the go-to for natural, production-ready voiceovers across multiple languages. It produces human-sounding narration suitable for explainer videos, podcasts, and any content that needs a clear, neutral voice.

    **Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS** is built for expressiveness. Use it when you want a narrator that responds to tone cues in your prompt — ideal for character dialogue, dramatic narration, and stylized creative content.

    <Warning>
      **ElevenLabs Multilingual v2** has a **450-character limit per generation**. For longer scripts, split your text across multiple Audio Nodes and connect them sequentially in your workflow.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Transcription">
    **ElevenLabs Scribe v2** converts speech to text with high accuracy. Feed it an audio file from an upstream node — or import a recording directly — and it outputs a clean transcript you can pipe into a Text Node for summarization, translation, or editing.

    Common use cases: subtitle generation, meeting notes, content indexing, and accessibility workflows.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sound Effects">
    **ElevenLabs Sound Effects** generates Foley and ambient audio from text descriptions. Describe the sound you need — a creaking door, crowd chatter, a UI click, rain on glass — and the model produces a short audio clip you can layer into your video output.

    Works well as a companion to video generation nodes when you want bespoke audio rather than a full music track.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Music">
    **ElevenLabs Music v1** composes original music from a text prompt. Describe the mood, tempo, instrumentation, or genre — or combine several descriptors — and the model returns a background track ready to drop under a video or use as a standalone asset.

    Typical generation time is 30 seconds. For longer compositions, chain multiple Music nodes together.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Output Format

<Info>
  All audio models output **MP3 at 128 kbps**. WAV and lossless formats are not currently available. If your downstream workflow requires uncompressed audio, export the MP3 and convert it in your DAW or audio editor.
</Info>
