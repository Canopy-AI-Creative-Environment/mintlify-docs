## Overview

The Audio Node handles all audio generation and processing within Canopy AI. It supports five distinct modes for different audio tasks.

## Modes

### Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Convert text into natural-sounding speech. Choose from a library of voices or clone a custom voice with a reference audio sample.

### Sound Effects (SFX)

Describe a sound effect in natural language and the AI generates it -- footsteps, explosions, ambient rain, mechanical whirring, and more.

### Music Generation

Generate original music tracks from text descriptions. Specify genre, tempo, mood, instruments, and duration.

### Transcription

Feed audio into the node to get accurate text transcription with timestamps and speaker identification.

### Lip Sync

Connect a video and an audio track to automatically sync lip movements to the audio. Useful for dubbing, voiceover replacement, and character animation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Mode">Choose your audio task from the mode dropdown at the top of the node.</Step>
  <Step title="Configure">Set model, voice (for TTS), or describe your desired output.</Step>
  <Step title="Connect and Run">Wire up inputs from upstream nodes and click **Run**.</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Chain TTS output into a Video Node with lip sync to create talking-head videos from a single text prompt.</Tip>
