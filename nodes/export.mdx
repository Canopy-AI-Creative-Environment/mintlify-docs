## Overview

The Export Node is the final step in your Canopy AI workflow. Connect any node's output to an Export Node to download the result or save it to an external service.

## Export Options

### Direct Download

Click **Download** on the Export Node to save the output file to your device. Supports all media types -- images (PNG, JPEG, WebP), video (MP4), audio (MP3, WAV), and text (TXT, Markdown).

### Google Drive Export

Connect your Google account in **Settings > Integrations** to enable one-click export to Google Drive. Choose a destination folder and the Export Node uploads automatically when the workflow completes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect">Wire any upstream node's output to the Export Node's input port.</Step>
  <Step title="Configure">Select file format, quality settings, and export destination.</Step>
  <Step title="Run">Execute the workflow -- the Export Node saves or downloads the final output.</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Export Nodes do not consume generation credits. You can add as many Export Nodes as needed to save outputs at different stages of your workflow.</Note>
