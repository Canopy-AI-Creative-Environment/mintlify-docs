## Overview

The Video Node generates dynamic video content using AI models. Feed it a text description, a starting image, or an audio track and get back a video clip ready for editing or export.

## Modes

### Text-to-Video

Describe the video you want in the prompt field. Specify camera movement, scene transitions, and visual style. Select duration and resolution from the node settings.

### Image-to-Video

Connect an Image Node output to animate a still image. The AI infers motion from the image content and your prompt -- specify camera moves, character actions, or environmental effects.

### Audio-to-Video

Feed an audio clip to generate video that matches the audio's rhythm, mood, and content. Useful for music visualizations and podcast content.

<Tip>For best results with image-to-video, use high-resolution source images and describe the desired motion explicitly in the prompt.</Tip>

## Settings

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Model** | Select from available video generation models |
| **Duration** | Clip length (model-dependent, typically 2-10 seconds) |
| **Aspect Ratio** | 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, or custom |

<Note>Video generation uses more credits than image generation. Chain shorter clips in the [Timeline Editor](/editor/timeline-editor) for longer sequences.</Note>
